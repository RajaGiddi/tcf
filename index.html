When We Meet
We are a non-denomintional telugu fellowship that seeks to honor Jesus Christ in the Telugu language in the Mechanicsburg area.
We seek to know Jesus Christ and lead people into the saving knowledge of the Lord Jesus Christ. We seek to disciple people to become disciple makers and walk in the gifts and fruits of the Holy Spirit
We meet every second Saturday of the month at 124 State Road, Mechanicsburg PA from 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM. Please come join us for fellowship and dinner after service.
